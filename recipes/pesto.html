Pesto Recipe

Description

This recipe comes from Samin Nosrat's cookbook Salt Fat Acid Heat. I've made pesto many different times in different ways, but this always turns out the best! Toasting the pine nuts and pounding them and the garlic in a morter and pestle makes all the difference.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 packed cups fresh basil leaves
  • 2 cloves garlic, pounded
  • 1/2 cup pine nuts, pounded
  • 3 1/2 ounces parmesan, finely grated
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Roughly chop the basil
  2. Pour half of the olive oil into food processor, then add basil and the rest of the olive oil
  3. Pulse food processor until basil has broken down and combined with the oil
  4. Transfer oil and basil mixture to a bowl. Add pine nuts, garlic, and parmesan a little bit at a time, tasting as you go.

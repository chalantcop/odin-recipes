One-Pot Tomato Pasta

Description

This recipe comes from the cookbook A Modern Way to Cook by Anna Jones. This method of cooking the pasta creates fewer dirty dishes, and the sauce created by boiling everything together is really tasty!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1 pound cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Zest of two lemons
  • 7 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspons kosher salt
  • 1 bunch spinach, chopped
  • Black pepper
  • Parmesan, for serving

Steps

  1. Bring a little over a quart of water to a boil (I like to use an electric kettle for this)
  2. While the water boils, add spaghetti, tomatoes, lemon zest, olive oil, and salt to a shallow pan large enough for the spaghetti to lay flat
  3. Pour boiling water into the pan, cover, and bring up to a boil again
  4. Once boiling, remove lid and continue to cook on high, turning the spaghetti with tongs every so often
  5. After about six minutes, add spinach. Continue to cook until almost no liquid remains.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and serve with parmesan.
